```
Label: AB = 5 cm (left side), BC = 12 cm (bottom), AC = 13 cm (hypotenuse). 
Mark right angle indicator at B.

Solution:

Step 1: Identify the sides. AB = 5 cm, BC = 12 cm, AC = 13 cm

Step 2: Check if these satisfy Pythagorean theorem. 
If ∠ABC = 90°, then AB² + BC² = AC²

Step 3: Calculate AB²: 5² = 25

Step 4: Calculate BC²: 12² = 144

Step 5: Add the squares: AB² + BC² = 25 + 144 = 169

Step 6: Calculate AC²: 13² = 169

Step 7: Compare: AB² + BC² = 169 = AC²
Since the equation is satisfied, by converse of Pythagorean theorem, ∠ABC = 90°

Final answer: The angle ABC is 90° (right angle)
```
